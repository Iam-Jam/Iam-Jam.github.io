Overview

My social media management work with Burnett Real Estate involved crafting visually captivating graphics and engaging content to promote properties and enhance brand presence across various social media platforms.

This multifaceted role encompassed graphic design by ensuring consistent branding and appealing visuals, Content creation is equally vital, requiring adeptness in crafting compelling property descriptions, informative captions, and strategic content calendars to drive engagement and reach.

Effective social media management entailed community engagement, ad campaign optimization, and analytics monitoring to foster relationships with followers, enhance visibility, and ultimately drive real estate sales.

In essence, this role combined creativity, strategic thinking, and a deep understanding of both real estate dynamics and social media trends to maximize online presence and promote properties effectively.
